Welcome to Pronto Weston

Your local destination for authentic Italian dining.

Welcome to Our Home

Our ethos is simple – classic Italian food designed to fill your table and be shared with family and friends. Coupled with our 300-year-old Neapolitan pizza recipe, regional Italian pasta, specialty delicatessen products, local and imported wines and a passion for what we do, we bring you PRONTO.

Authenticity, Certified.

Did you know that our restaurants are the only two in the Canberra region certified by the Associazione Verace Pizza Napoletana (AVPN) in Napoli? This is our commitment to true, Neapolitan pizza.
