Our Story

Welcome to Pronto, where 300 years of Neapolitan pizza-making tradition comes alive in the heart of Canberra's communities. Brothers Josh and Jon Kosteski have created two authentic Italian dining destinations – Pronto Queanbeyan and our newest location, Pronto Weston in Cooleman Court – each serving as a family-friendly haven where every meal feels like a warm hug from an Italian Nonna.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to authenticity: we're proud to be the only two restaurants in the Canberra region certified by the Associazione Verace Pizza Napoletana (AVPN) in Naples, ensuring that every pizza we serve meets the strictest standards for True Neapolitan Pizza.

At Pronto, we believe food is meant to be shared, and our menu is thoughtfully designed around classic Italian dishes that fill your table and bring family and friends together. From our hand-stretched pizzas fired in traditional ovens to our house-made pasta and carefully crafted shared plates, every dish tells a story of Italian tradition and family recipes passed down through generations.

Both our Queanbeyan and Weston communities have welcomed us with open arms, and we're honored to serve authentic Italian cuisine that encourages conversation, connection, and the generous spirit that defines true Italian hospitality. Whether you're joining us for a casual lunch, a family dinner, or a special celebration, every visit to Pronto is an invitation to become part of our extended family – Benvenuti alla famiglia.
