Authentic Italian Dining in the Heart of Rome

Welcome to Belissimo, where traditional Italian flavors meet modern dining excellence. Located in the vibrant Trastevere district of Rome, we bring you the authentic taste of Italy with every dish.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for authentic Italian cuisine, Belissimo has been serving Rome's finest traditional dishes since 2015. Our skilled chefs use only the freshest local ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

What We Offer

  • Handcrafted Pasta - Made fresh daily using traditional techniques
  • Wood-Fired Pizza - Crispy, authentic pizzas from our traditional stone oven
  • Seasonal Specialties - Featuring the best of Roman and Italian regional cuisine
  • Fine Wine Selection - Carefully curated Italian wines to complement your meal
  • Warm Atmosphere - Perfect for romantic dinners, family gatherings, or business meetings
