Our Story

Established in 1996, Kitchen Garden at Rodney's has become a cherished meeting place for business gatherings, mothers groups, family celebrations, and relaxed lunches among friends.

Our executive chef, Nathan Kleinig, brings exceptional expertise from his time as executive chef at Parliament House, along with experience from many renowned restaurants throughout Canberra.
