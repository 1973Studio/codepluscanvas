About Us

Kitchen Garden at Rodney’s is more than just a café — it’s a true family affair. Located in the heart of Pialligo, the café is proudly operated by husband and wife team Emma Toll and Nathan Kleinig, while the adjoining nursery is owned by Emma’s parents, Rodney and Cheryl Toll, who began the business over 50 years ago and remain actively involved to this day.

Emma, who grew up on the property, has always had a deep love of food, plants, and the unique way they connect people. With a background in horticulture and international experience, she brings a thoughtful touch to the café’s atmosphere and growth.

Nathan, a seasoned executive chef who trained in some of Canberra’s most iconic kitchens including Parliament House, leads a passionate team of 30. Since taking over the café 15 years ago, Emma and Nathan have grown it from a small operation into a thriving destination for business lunches, family gatherings, and friends catching up over seasonal, farm-to-table fare. With deep roots and big plans, this dynamic duo continues to nurture the café as part of a vibrant, multigenerational family business.
