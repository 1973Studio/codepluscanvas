Frequently Asked Questions

What technology do you use?

We specialize in modern web technologies including HTML, CSS (Tailwind), and JavaScript for the front-end. We use serverless architecture with platforms like Netlify and Firebase for robust, scalable backend solutions.

How does your single package work?

We provide a full-service solution with a single monthly payment. This covers everything from the initial custom design and development to hosting, ongoing support, and maintenance. There are no large upfront costs and no lock-in contracts.

Who owns the website and content?

You do. All the content, imagery, and intellectual property are 100% yours. We simply provide the platform and services to bring it to life and keep it running smoothly.
